BACK TO THE TOP

One Bradford is a division of East Bay Newspapers, founded in 1837 in Bristol, Rhode Island.

Originally created to support the non-advertising, but diverse marketing needs of our clients, One Bradford expanded to be a full service digital marketing firm. Our clients span all industries and are located throughout the East Coast. We provide web, search, and social marketing services, with our greatest strength lying in our strategy. We work with our clients to build a customized program to meet their unique goals.

In 2013, EBN acquired The Chatter Group, a social media consultancy, whose founder, Kirsten DiChiappari is now Managing Director of One Bradford. Kirsten has taught workshops for the RI Economic Development Corporation, the Small Business Development Center, the Small Business Association, and numerous Chambers of Commerce through Rhode Island. Currently, Kirsten sits on the Board of the Bristol Warren Education Foundation. Learn more about Kirsten here.
